Headings and Paragraphs

  • Heading tags: <h1> - <h6>

    1. <h1>Heading 1 </h1>
    2. <h2>Sub heading 2 </h2>
    3. <h3>Sub heading 3 </h3>

  • Paragraph Tags: <p>

    1. <p>This is my first paragraph</p>
    2. <p>This is my second paragraph</p>

  • Sections: <div> and <span>

    1. <div style="background: skyblue;">
    2.       This is a div
    3. </div>
